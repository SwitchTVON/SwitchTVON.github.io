Discover

Press and hold the button to open a menu in which you can select the filter.

The filter only contains the categories for genres, countries, languages and ratings for the range of TV shows you have been browsing. The further you browse horizontally through the TV shows, the more categories appear in the list. If you select a category, the range of TV shows is filtered according to this category. All filters add up. This works not only for the trending TV shows, but also for the trending movies, now playing movies and upcoming movies. Not for the trending people.

  • "Alphabetical" shows the entries sorted alphabetically by title
  • "Release date" shows the entries sorted by the release date of the movie or the next episode of the series
  • "Selection date" shows the entries sorted by the date on which they were marked as favorites

Trend of "the day" shows the current daily trend for the trendy series, films and people, while the trend of "the week" shows a longer-term weekly trend.

  • If a movie or at least one episode of a series is in the watchlist, the thumbnail image is marked with an eye in the top left-hand corner
  • If a film, series or person is in the favorites, the thumbnail image is marked with a star in the top right-hand corner

The eye with the date indicates that at least one episode of the TV show has been marked as watched in the watchlist. The date shows when the episode was watched.

Privacy Policy

Switch TV ON takes the protection of your data very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the application uses the personal data it collects from you when you use the application.

We only collect the data that is required for the app to function:
The names of movies, TV shows and person are only stored on the iPhone for the history of search results and can be removed by the user at any time. Likewise, the tagging of a TV series, movie or person, as well as the tracking of an episode or movie as watched.

Switch TV ON uses the TMDB service as a data source, i.e. the search or update of TV series, films and people is carried out via this service.
